# CorePrep for CMA — Frequently Asked Questions

## General

**What is CorePrep for CMA?**
CorePrep for CMA is a study companion app for the CMA (Certified Management Accountant) exam. It covers both Part 1 (Financial Planning, Performance, and Analytics) and Part 2 (Strategic Financial Management), offering structured study content, multiple-choice questions, interactive simulators, and a study planner — all designed to help you prepare effectively.

**Who made CorePrep for CMA?**
CorePrep for CMA was developed by Maher I. Ladsak, CMA, CPA(PH) in collaboration with Rork.com. All rights reserved.

**What platforms does CorePrep for CMA support?**
CorePrep for CMA is currently available on iOS.

**Is it free?**
CorePrep for CMA uses a freemium model. Part 1 Units 1 & 2, their MCQs, and the Master Budget Simulator (default preset) are free. All other content requires a one-time lifetime purchase to unlock.

## Study Content

**How is the content organized?**
Content is organized hierarchically: Books → Units → Subunits → Topics. Part 1 covers Financial Planning, Performance, and Analytics. Part 2 covers Strategic Financial Management.

**How do I navigate between units and topics?**
Use the **Units** tab to browse all units. Tap a unit to see its subunits, then tap a subunit to view its topics. You can also use the **Search** tab to find specific content.

**How do I mark a topic as read?**
Tap the checkmark toggle next to any topic to mark it as read. Your reading progress is tracked per unit and displayed on the Home dashboard.

**Can I bookmark topics?**
Yes. Tap the bookmark icon on any topic or unit summary to save it. Bookmarked items appear on the Home screen for quick access.

**What are unit summaries?**
Each unit includes a summary view that provides a high-level overview of the unit's content.

**What does "Interactive" mean on a topic?**
Topics tagged as "Interactive" link directly to one of the built-in financial simulators, allowing you to explore concepts hands-on.

## Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQ)

**How do I practice MCQs?**
Go to the **MCQ** tab. Select a unit to start answering questions. Questions are presented one at a time with four answer choices (A–D).

**How does the MCQ system work?**
Tap on answer choices to interact. Selecting a wrong answer eliminates it and reveals its explanation. Selecting the correct answer confirms it. Each choice has its own detailed explanation so you learn why each option is right or wrong.

**Is my first attempt tracked?**
Yes. The app tracks whether your first answer for each question was correct. This is reflected in your accuracy statistics.

**Can I navigate between questions?**
Yes. Use the previous/next buttons at the bottom, or tap the question list icon to jump to any question. Questions are color-coded by status: correct (green), incorrect (red), or unanswered.

**Can I redo a unit's MCQs?**
Yes. Use the **Redo** option to reset a unit's MCQ progress and reshuffle the answer choices for a fresh attempt.

**Can I review my answered questions?**
Yes. A **Review** mode lets you browse all answered questions for a unit, with search highlighting support.

**Where can I see my MCQ stats?**
The MCQ Dashboard shows overall progress and per-unit statistics including questions answered, correct/wrong counts, and accuracy percentage.

**What about the essay section?**
The MCQ Dashboard includes a note about the essay section of the CMA exam. The app focuses on multiple-choice preparation.

## Simulators

**What are simulators?**
Simulators are interactive financial calculators built into the app. They let you explore key CMA concepts by adjusting inputs and seeing results in real time.

**What simulators are available?**
There are 11 simulators:
1. **Ratio Analysis** — Calculate and interpret key financial ratios
2. **DuPont Decomposition** — Break down Return on Equity into its components
3. **WACC Builder** — Build and calculate Weighted Average Cost of Capital
4. **Cash Conversion Cycle** — Analyze the cash-to-cash timeline
5. **CVP / Break-Even Analysis** — Explore cost-volume-profit relationships
6. **Sensitivity Analysis** — Test how changes in variables affect outcomes
7. **Capital Budgeting** — Evaluate investment decisions (NPV, IRR, etc.)
8. **ERP Data Flow** — Visualize enterprise resource planning data flows
9. **Variance Analysis** — Analyze budget vs. actual performance variances
10. **Flexible Budget** — Create and compare flexible budgets
11. **Master Budget** — Build a complete master budget (with default and zero-based presets)

**Are simulators free?**
The Master Budget Simulator (default preset) is free. All other simulators and the zero-based preset require the premium unlock.

**Are my simulator inputs saved?**
Yes. All simulator inputs are automatically saved and restored when you return.

**Can I reset a simulator?**
Yes. Each simulator has a reset button to clear all inputs back to defaults.

> ⚠️ Simulators are for educational purposes only and should not be used for actual financial decision-making.

## Study Plan

**What is the Study Plan?**
The Study Plan lets you create a personalized study schedule for the CMA exam. Access it from the **Plan** tab.

**How do I set up a study plan?**
Set your overall book schedule by choosing start and end dates, then configure your weekly availability (hours per week and blocked days).

**Can the app create a schedule for me?**
Yes. Use the **Auto-Schedule** feature to automatically distribute units across your schedule based on your availability. You can also manually set start and end dates for individual units.

**How does the calendar view work?**
The calendar displays color-coded dots showing which units are scheduled on each day. Tap any date to see the units scheduled for that day and their completion percentage.

**What happens if I fall behind?**
Units past their end date with incomplete progress are flagged as **overdue**. The overdue count is displayed as a badge on the Plan tab and on your app icon.

## Search

**How do I search for content?**
Tap the **Search** tab and type your query. You can search across two modes:
- **Study Units** — Searches topic titles, bodies, excerpts, and unit summaries
- **MCQ** — Searches question stems, correct answers, and explanations

**Are search results highlighted?**
Yes. Matching text is highlighted in the search results and when you navigate to the matching content.

**Does search include locked content?**
No. Locked (premium) content is excluded from search results until you unlock it.

## Text-to-Speech (TTS)

**Can the app read content aloud?**
Yes. CorePrep includes a built-in Text-to-Speech feature that reads topic content aloud sentence by sentence.

**How do I use TTS?**
Tap the speaker icon while viewing a topic. Use the play, pause, and stop controls to manage playback.

**Can I change the reading speed?**
Yes. There are 7 speed levels ranging from 0.85x to 1.15x. You can change speed during playback or while paused.

**How do I enable or disable the TTS button?**
Go to **Settings → Display** and toggle the TTS button visibility.

## Backup & Restore

**How do I back up my data?**
Go to **Settings** and use the **Export** option. This creates a `.coreprep` file containing all your progress, study plan, MCQ answers, bookmarks, recent items, simulator inputs, and settings.

**What is included in a backup?**
Everything: reading progress, MCQ answers, bookmarks, recent items, study plan, simulator inputs, and display settings (TTS speed, font size, appearance mode).

**How do I restore from a backup?**
Go to **Settings** and use the **Import** option. Select a `.coreprep` or `.json` backup file from your device. A confirmation dialog shows backup details (date, topics read, MCQs answered) before restoring.

> ⚠️ Restoring a backup will override all existing progress. This action is irreversible.

**How do I share or save my backup file?**
When you export, the iOS Share Sheet appears, allowing you to save the file to iCloud Drive, Google Drive, email it to yourself, or share via any other method.

## Settings & Display

**Can I change the font size?**
Yes. In **Settings → Display**, switch between Normal and Large font size.

**Can I change the appearance mode?**
Yes. Choose between System, Light, or Dark mode in **Settings → Display**.

**Can I reset my progress?**
Yes. In **Settings**, you can reset individually:
- Unit reading progress
- MCQ progress
- Study plan
- Simulator inputs
- Or reset all at once

## Purchase & Premium

**What do I get with the premium unlock?**
Access to all units (Part 1 Units 3–6 and all Part 2 units), all MCQ questions, all simulators, and the zero-based Master Budget preset.

**Is it a subscription?**
No. CorePrep for CMA offers a **one-time lifetime purchase**. Pay once and access all content forever.

**How do I restore my purchase on a new device?**
Go to the Paywall screen or **Settings** and tap **Restore Purchases**. Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

**What if my purchase isn't recognized?**
Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase, then tap Restore Purchases. If the issue persists, contact support.

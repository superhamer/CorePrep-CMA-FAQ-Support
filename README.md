# CorePrep CMA — FAQ & Support

**CorePrep for CMA** is a professional study companion app for the CMA® (Certified Management Accountant) exam, available on iOS.

---

## General

**What is CorePrep for CMA?**
CorePrep for CMA helps candidates prepare for the CMA® exam with curated study content, practice questions, interactive simulators, and a built-in study planner — all within a single native iOS experience.

**Who developed this app?**
CorePrep for CMA was developed by Maher I. Ladsak, CMA, CPA(PH). All rights reserved.

**Is the app free?**
CorePrep uses a freemium model. A selection of introductory content is available at no cost. Full access to all study materials, practice questions, and simulators is available through a one-time lifetime purchase.

---

## Study Content

**What exam parts are covered?**
The app covers both Part 1 (Financial Planning, Performance, and Analytics) and Part 2 (Strategic Financial Management) in their entirety.

**How do I track my progress?**
The app provides reading progress tracking, bookmarking, and a home dashboard that summarizes your study activity across all units.

---

## Practice Questions

**How do practice questions work?**
The app features a proprietary MCQ engine with per-choice explanations, first-attempt accuracy tracking, and intelligent review modes. Questions are designed to reinforce understanding of why each answer is correct or incorrect.

**Can I track my performance?**
Yes. A dedicated dashboard provides detailed analytics on your question-answering performance across all units.

---

## Interactive Simulators

**What simulators are included?**
CorePrep includes a suite of proprietary interactive financial simulators covering key CMA® exam topics such as ratio analysis, cost of capital, budgeting, variance analysis, cost-volume-profit relationships, and more. These simulators are purpose-built for CMA® exam preparation and go beyond simple calculators.

**Are simulator inputs saved?**
Yes. All inputs persist automatically between sessions.

> All financial simulators and calculators in this app are provided for educational and illustration purposes only. Results are based on simplified assumptions and should not be used as the sole basis for any financial, investment, or business decision. Always consult a qualified professional before making financial decisions.

---

## Study Planner

**Can I create a study schedule?**
Yes. The built-in planner supports custom scheduling with calendar integration, weekly availability configuration, auto-scheduling, and overdue tracking with badge notifications.

---

## Search

**Can I search across all content?**
Yes. The app supports full-text search across study materials and practice questions, with result highlighting.

---

## Text-to-Speech

**Can the app read content aloud?**
Yes. A built-in TTS feature reads topic content sentence by sentence with adjustable playback speed.

---

## Backup & Restore

**Can I back up my progress?**
Yes. The app supports full data export and import via a proprietary `.coreprep` file format. Backups include all progress, settings, and study data, and can be shared via the iOS Share Sheet.

> Restoring a backup will override all existing progress. This action is irreversible.

---

## Purchase & Premium

**Is it a subscription?**
No. CorePrep for CMA offers a **one-time lifetime purchase**. Pay once, access everything forever.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Tap **Restore Purchases** in the app. Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device signed in with the same account.
